My Favourite Time of Year

Christmas is one of my favourite times of the year. There are many things that I love about Christmas, including:
  • the snow
  • holidays
  • food
  • the spirit

The cold, snowy weather is one of my favourite parts of Christmas. I also love the holiday because everyone has the day off to celebrate, and be in the Christmas spirit. Christmas dinner is always one of my favourite parts too as it is a time of celebration with family (and the food is always delicious!).
