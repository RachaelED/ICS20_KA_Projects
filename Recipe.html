Recipe #1 - Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 24
Ingredients Quantity
butter, softened 1 cup
white sugar 1 cup
brown sugar, packed 1 cup
eggs 2
vanilla extract 2 tsp
baking soda 1 tsp
hot water 2 tsp
salt 1/2 tsp
all purpose flour 3 cups
semi sweet chocolate chips 2 cups
chopped walnuts 1 cup

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Step 2: Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts.

Step 3: Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.

Step 4: Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.

Recipe #2 - Chocolate Cake

  • Time: 45-60 minutes
  • Serves: 12
Ingredients Quantity
sugar 2 cup
all purpose flour 1 3/4 cup
Hershey's cocoa 3/4 cup
baking powder 1 1/2 tsp
baking soda 1 1/2 tsp
salt 1 tsp
eggs 2
milk 1 cup
vegetable oil 1 cup
vanilla extract 2 tsp
boiling water 1 cup

Step 1: Heat oven to 350Â°F. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.

Step 2: Stir together sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt in large bowl. Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla; beat on medium speed of mixer 2 minutes. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin). Pour batter into prepared pans.

Step 3: Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool 10 minutes; remove from pans to wire racks. Cool completely. Frost with "PERFECTLY CHOCOLATE" CHOCOLATE FROSTING.

Recipe #3 - Banana Muffins

  • Time: 35 minutes
  • Serves:12
Ingredients Quantity
all purpose flour 1 cup
baking powder 1 tsp
baking soda 1 tsp
salt 1/2 tsp
bananas, mashed 3
white sugar 3/4 cup
egg 1
butter, melted 1/3 cup

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Coat muffin pans with non-stick spray, or use paper liners. Sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt; set aside.

Step 2: Combine bananas, sugar, egg, and melted butter in a large bowl. Fold in flour mixture, and mix until smooth. Scoop into muffin pans.

Step 3: Bake in preheated oven. Bake mini muffins for 10 to 15 minutes, and large muffins for 25 to 30 minutes. Muffins will spring back when lightly tapped.

Variation: Turn into loaves instead of muffins.

Step 1: Create batter as shown above.

Step 2: Bake 60 minutes or until toothpick inserted in the middle of the loaf comes out clean.
